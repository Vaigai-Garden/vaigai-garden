Our Story

How we brought the legendary flavours of Tamil Nadu's food capital to the busy streets of Bengaluru.

Welcome to Vaigai Garden

At Vaigai Garden, we don't just cook — we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of South India. What began as a single kitchen dream rooted in the traditions of Madurai has grown into a beloved Bengaluru institution with two thriving branches.

We realized that while Bengaluru had many South Indian restaurants, the specific, bold, and aromatic spices of Madurai were missing. We decided to bring the Legendary Bun Parotta, the spicy Madurai Salna, and the fragrant Dum Biriyani to our local community.

Every ingredient is handpicked, every spice is perfectly balanced, and every guest is treated like family. Join us and taste the difference that genuine passion, authentic recipes, and over a decade of culinary dedication makes.
