Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Served with Love.

At Vaigai Garden, we don't just cook — we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of South India. What began as a single kitchen dream rooted in the traditions of Madurai has grown into a beloved Bengaluru institution with two thriving branches.

Every ingredient is handpicked, every spice is perfectly balanced, and every guest is treated like family. Whether you're stopping by for a quick breakfast Dosa or settling in for a full Biriyani feast, we promise a meal that nourishes both body and soul.

Join us and taste the difference that genuine passion, authentic recipes, and over a decade of culinary dedication makes.
